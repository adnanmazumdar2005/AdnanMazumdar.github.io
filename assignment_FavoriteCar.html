MY DREAM CAR

PORSCHE 992 911 GT3.

My dream car is the Porsche 992 911 GT3.
For almost 25 years the 911 GT3 has accepted only the limits of physics and left the ordinary far behind. Motorsport-tested design and maximum performance ensure it unwaveringly bridges the boundary between the racetrack and the road. It stands as a testament to Porsche's unparalleled design and engineering prowess.

Highlights.

High-reving naturally aspirated engine.

The powerful, naturally aspirated, flat-six engine underlines 9,000 revs/min, staying true to its motorsport origins - for a driving experience beyond the norm.

Short gear ratios.

The 7-speed Porsche Doppelkupplung (PDK) and the manual 6-speed GT sports gearbox transform propulsion into adrenaline rushes: with fast gear changes and sporty short gear ratios.

Weissach package.

Wolf in sheep's cloth8ing: for the first time, the 911 GT3 is available with the Weissach package as andn option and, thanks to numerous elements made of Carbon-Fibre Reinforced Plastic (CFRP), it weighs less - delivering more performance on the asphalt.

Folding lightweight sports bucket seats.

The leightweight sports bucket seats (folding) made of Carbon-Fibre Reinforced Plastic (CFRP) in a Carbon-weave finish (high-gloss) consistently continue the lightweight construction concept of the 911 GT3 in the inerior.

Aerodynamics and design.

More striking, more functional, more powerful: the sharpened design further improves aerodynamics, and with front lights integrated into the headlights, it creates additional space for side cooling air openings. This design ensures exceptional performance, with the standard GT3 generating an impressive 860 kg of downforce and the RS variant pushing the limits even further, producing a staggering 1,000 kg of downforce.
